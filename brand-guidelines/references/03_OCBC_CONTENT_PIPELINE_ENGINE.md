## 1. The Execution Workflow

Every content request runs through five stages. Skipping a stage is how off-brand content ships.

1. **Topic intake.** State the financial topic in one sentence and name the segment: NYALA (individual Financial Fitness) or Nyala Bisnis (entrepreneur/UMKM). One topic, one segment, per request. Mixing segments dilutes both.
2. **Angle selection.** Run the topic through the Angle Differentiation Engine (Section 2) before writing anything. Do not draft copy first and retrofit an angle.
3. **Format assignment.** Match angle to platform using the table below: not every angle fits every platform.
4. **Draft against Module 1 and Module 2.** Copy is checked against the Tone of Voice Matrix and Copywriting Rules (Module 1); visuals are checked against the Color/Type/Layout rules (Module 2).
5. **Quick Voice Check.** Run the four-question check from Module 1, Section 5, before calling anything final.

| Angle | Best Platform | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Contrarian / myth-correction | LinkedIn text post, IG carousel slide 1 | Needs room for a claim plus evidence; too dense for a 20-second Reel |
| Step-by-step / how-to | IG Carousel | Carousel's swipe mechanic is built for sequence |
| Fast-paced / hook-driven | Reels / Stories | Needs a single idea and a strong first 1.5 seconds, not a multi-step process |
| Data visualization / trend | LinkedIn Card | Landscape frame suits a chart-plus-headline unit |

## 2. Angle Differentiation Engine

One financial topic always produces three distinct outputs, never one message copy-pasted into three shapes. Below is the reference case: **SBN Ritel** (Indonesian government retail bonds).

### Format A: Contrarian LinkedIn Post
**Angle:** Correct a widespread misconception with evidence.

> **Headline:** "SBN Ritel Bukan Investasi Bebas Risiko: Ini yang Perlu Kamu Tahu Dulu"
>
> Kebanyakan orang membeli SBN Ritel karena dijamin negara, lalu berhenti mikir di situ. Tapi "dijamin negara" hanya menghilangkan risiko gagal bayar (default risk), bukan risiko lainnya.
>
> Ada dua risiko yang tetap melekat: risiko likuiditas (kamu tidak bisa cair sebelum jatuh tempo tanpa mekanisme pasar sekunder yang kadang tipis), dan risiko kesempatan (kupon tetap berarti kamu kehilangan potensi kenaikan kalau suku bunga acuan naik setelah kamu beli).
>
> Data OJK 2025 mencatat rata-rata tenor SBN Ritel yang dipegang investor ritel adalah 2-3 tahun. Mayoritas menahan hingga jatuh tempo, bukan karena strategi, tapi karena tidak tahu opsi keluar mereka terbatas.
>
> Sebelum beli SBN Ritel berikutnya, tanyakan satu hal ke diri sendiri: apakah dana ini benar-benar bisa kamu kunci selama tenor penuh? Kalau jawabannya ragu, itu bukan produk yang salah, itu alokasi yang salah.

### Format B: Step-by-Step IG Carousel
**Angle:** Teach the mechanics, sequentially, for a first-time buyer.

- **Slide 1 (Cover, H1):** "Cara Beli SBN Ritel Pertama Kali, Tanpa Salah Langkah"
- **Slide 2:** Apa itu SBN Ritel dalam satu kalimat + siapa yang boleh beli (WNI, usia 17+, punya rekening dana & rekening surat berharga).
- **Slide 3:** Langkah 1: pilih mitra distribusi resmi (bank atau platform terdaftar OJK).
- **Slide 4:** Langkah 2: buka rekening dana nasabah (RDN) dan rekening surat berharga jika belum punya.
- **Slide 5:** Langkah 3: pesan di masa penawaran, minimum dan kelipatan pemesanan.
- **Slide 6:** Yang sering dilewatkan: cek jadwal kupon dan tanggal jatuh tempo sebelum konfirmasi, bukan sesudah.
- **Slide 7 (CTA):** "Cek simulasi kupon SBN Ritel kamu di ocbc.id/fitness" + swipe indicator.

### Format C: Fast-Paced Reels Script
**Angle:** One sharp correction, delivered in under 30 seconds.

```
[0:00–0:02] ON SCREEN TEXT: "SBN Ritel itu BUKAN tabungan."
SPOKEN: "Kalau kamu mikir SBN Ritel sama kayak nabung, stop dulu."

[0:03–0:10]
SPOKEN: "SBN itu kamu minjemin uang ke negara, dan negara bayar
bunga tetap ke kamu tiap bulan. Tapi uangnya kekunci sampai jatuh
tempo, nggak bisa diambil kapan aja kayak tabungan."

[0:11–0:18]
SPOKEN: "Jadi sebelum beli, tanya ke diri sendiri: dana ini beneran
nggak kepake dalam 2-3 tahun ke depan? Kalau masih ragu, taruh di
instrumen yang lebih likuid dulu."

[0:19–0:25]
ON SCREEN TEXT: "Cek Financial Fitness Index kamu, link di bio."
SPOKEN: "Cek dulu profil finansial kamu sebelum milih instrumen,
link-nya ada di bio."
```

**What makes these three genuinely different outputs, not one message resized:** the LinkedIn post carries the argument and the evidence for a reader who wants to be convinced. The carousel carries the procedure for a reader who has already decided and needs the mechanics. The Reel carries a single corrective idea for a reader who hasn't decided anything yet and needs a reason to keep watching. Each format earns its length; none of them is a shrunk or stretched version of another.

## 3. The Three-Step Prompt Formula

Use this exact structure to invoke this skill for any new topic, in any future session:

**Step 1: State the topic and segment.**
> "Topic: [financial product/behavior]. Segment: NYALA (individual) or Nyala Bisnis (entrepreneur/UMKM)."

**Step 2: State the angle, or ask for all three.**
> "Give me the Contrarian LinkedIn post, the Step-by-Step IG Carousel, and the Fast-Paced Reels script for this topic."
(Or name one format if only one is needed.)

**Step 3: State the constraint, if any.**
> "Constraint: [e.g., must cite OJK data / must target first-jobbers / must tie to Nyala Bisnis's cash-flow angle / 15-second Reel max]."

**Full worked command:**
> "Topic: dana darurat (emergency fund). Segment: Financial Fitness. Give me the Contrarian LinkedIn post, the Step-by-Step IG Carousel, and the Fast-Paced Reels script. Constraint: must cite a specific multiple of monthly expenses as the target, and must address the excuse 'gaji pas-pasan, gimana bisa nabung.'"

Every output produced through this formula is checked against Module 1 (voice, banned language) and Module 2 (color, type, layout) before it ships.
